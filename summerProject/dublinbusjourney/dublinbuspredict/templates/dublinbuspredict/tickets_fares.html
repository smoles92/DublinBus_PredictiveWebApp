Ticket & Fare Information

We provide accurate fare information for every type of Dublin Bus Customer. Check out the most up to date pricing below before setting out on your journey.

Adult Tickets
Our Adult tickets are suitable for everyday commuters, with prices separated by the number of stages that the user desires.

Stages Leap Card Cash Fare
Stage 1-4 €1.50 €2.00
Stage 4-13 €2.05 €2.70
Over 13 Stages €2.60 €3.30
Xpresso €2.85 €3.60
Route 90 €1.50 €2.00

Students can enjoy some of our more favourable rates with the use of a valid Student ID.

Stages Leap Card Cash Fare

Child Tickets
Children under the age of 4 can travel free on Dublin Bus, while those between 4-18 can take advantage of lower Child or School cash fares, with further savings by using a valid Child Leap Card.

Stages Leap Card (Under 19 yrs) Cash Fare (Under 16 yrs)
School Hours (All Stages) €0.79 €1.00
Stage 1-7 €0.90 €1.15
Over 7 Stages €1.10 €1.40
Xpresso €1.20 €1.50
Route 90 €0.90 €1.15
