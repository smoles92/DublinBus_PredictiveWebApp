Connection Information

Dublin is well-connected by numerous operators, serving both the City Centre and suburban areas.

Taxis
Dublin City & Suburbs are supported by many well-established Taxis services. Most provide Real-Time tracking of your taxi which allow you to track your journey time from start to finish. For fare information, contact some of our Sponsors: Lynk, and MyTaxi.

DART
In Dublin, the electric rail system called the DART (Dublin Area Rapid Transit) is a quick and easy way to get around Dublin. It runs along the coast of the Irish Sea from Malahide or Howth in north County Dublin soutwards as far as Greystones, Co Wicklow.

Luas
Luas is a light-weight Tram serving Dublin City Centre and Suburbs. With two tram lines currently, Luas expand with the opening of Luas Cross-city at the end of 2017. 34.2 million passengers were carried in 2016. On average, 90,000 passengers are carried daily.
